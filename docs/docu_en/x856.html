Production of the forms

The creation of the forms happens with the programFormEditor . First becomes the DB-schema sample/uni.xmldbschema Loaded File->DB of schema shop . Automatic creation of DB-forms happens with Tools->Form assistant . The created forms can designed with a GUI-editor by command buttonForm Edit . The finished forms are in sample/uni.xmlforms.
